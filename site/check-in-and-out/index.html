Parking.

The driveway can fit up to four cars, depending on their size. If you need more space, use the free public parking lot across the street from the house. Special Note: “First Fridays” is an event put on by North Beach every first Friday of the month between May and October. If you arrive after 5pm on one of these First Fridays, the road to our house will be blocked off and you’ll need to park at one of the public lots indicated on the map below with yellow stars. We hope you enjoy these events and sorry for any inconvenience!

Self check in with smart lock.

Your group’s personalized code will be sent prior to your arrival, and will be active from your check in time (3pm on day of arrival) to your check out time (10am on day of departure), unless otherwise arranged.

Enter the code on the keypad on the door facing the driveway for entry.

Check out is at 10am.

We hope you enjoyed your stay! Just 3 things we ask upon checkout:

  1. Please clean kitchen and put dishes in dishwasher. (If you used the grill, please clean that too)

  2. Take trash and recycling to the bins in the driveway.

  3. Please bring in outdoor cushions or put the furniture covers on.

Ensuring food and trash is removed helps us avoid pests in the house and difficult to remove smells for our next guests. Thank you.
